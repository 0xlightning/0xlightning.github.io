## Background Effects

### Add:

* Soft radial gradients
* Noise texture overlay
* Floating chess pieces
* Tiny grid patterns
* Blurred circular glow elements

### Avoid:

* Strong particles
* Heavy 3D scenes
* Overly dark cyberpunk themes
* Complex backgrounds
